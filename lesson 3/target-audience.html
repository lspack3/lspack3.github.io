Target Audience

The target audience are locals to the Preston and surrounding areas and travelers.

Personas

42 year old male who works in Rexburg and has to commute early in the moring. He needs this application so that he can check the weather and road conditions before heading out to work.

29 year old young mother. She has 3 children that love to play outside. She needs this application to check the weather for the day so that her children are properly dressed and so she knows if they can go to the park.

Senarios

  • A local farmer needs to know if the storm coming this week will bring hail or snow so that he can protect his crop.
  • A scout leader wants to check the weather for clear skies next weekend for the scout campout.
  • A teenager would like to know how much snow is expected tomorrow so that they can go sledding with their friends.
  • A mother is hoping it doesn't rain this weekend because she has a play date set up for her toddler at the park.
  • A conserned dog owner wants to know if the storm coming this week will bring thunder and lightning, which frightens her pup.
