The Temple Inn & Suites

Rooms Offered

Standard 1 Queen

Our standard 1 queen bedroom is a classic place to stay a night or two while you are visiting the temple. There is a mini fridge and microwave in the room for your convenience.

Standard 2 Queen

The standard 2 queen bedroom is perfect for a family or group of friends. A pull out couch, mini fridge and microwave are in the room for your convenience.

Deluxe 2 Queen

Our deliteful deluxe 2 queen has plenty of space for you and your family or friends. In addition to the microwave and mini fridge, we have a pull-out couch and an extra sink, mirror and closet. It has everything you need for your visit.

Deluxe 1 Queen 2 Twin

This room has a comfy 1 queen and 2 twin beds. It's perfect for a family with children with space and all the amenities you need while you visit the temple. A mini fridge and microwave are included.

Suite 1 King

Our 1 king bed suite is perfect for long term temple patrons or for newly weds on their honeymoon. It's much like a studio apartment. It has lots of space and a kitchenette with dishes, silverware, and everything else you could need while you stay with us.

Suite 1 King 2 Twin

This suite is perfect for temple patrons with children. This suite has a room for the children with 2 twin beds and 2 dressers. The king bed is in the main room along with the television and couch. There is a kitchenette with all the necessities you will need as well as a simple dining table.
