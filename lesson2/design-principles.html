Design Principles

Principle 1: Contrast

Odd Pears

Odd Pears Website

Odd Pears is a website that specializes in selling unique and very colorful socks. Their website is a great example of contrast in design. The salmon pink tags on the dark blue banner stand out on the white background. Not to mention all the other bright colors and fun designs on the socks that just pop. The eye can't help but be drawn to all the action happening with the contrast on this site!
